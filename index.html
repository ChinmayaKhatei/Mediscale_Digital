Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about medical advertising rules, budgets, and support workflows.

The National Medical Commission (NMC) restricts doctors from making exaggerated claims or promising guaranteed results. Our campaigns focus purely on clinical education, surgeon credentials, infrastructure specifications, and patient awareness guides, keeping the copy 100% professional and compliant.
For dental implant and ortho clinics, we suggest starting with at least ₹30,000–₹40,000 per month. For high-ticket eye care surgeries (LASIK/Cataracts), a budget of ₹50,000+ per month allows Google and Meta algorithms to optimize for surgical candidates effectively.
Whenever a patient fills out an ad inquiry form, our system triggers a custom WhatsApp conversation. If the patient responds or requests a callback, our healthcare support desk contacts them within 60 seconds (during business hours) to pre-qualify their treatment history, budget, and timing, before forwarding them directly to your front desk.
